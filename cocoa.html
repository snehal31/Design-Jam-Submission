COCOA COFFEE BEANS

In addition to the antioxidant benefits of cocoa, eating cocoa in the form of dark chocolate may be beneficial for your heart. Cocoa flavanols may benefit your cardiovascular health by decreasing blood pressure.

A meta-analysis, performed by researchers from the Cardiology Department at a German University, found that participants who consumed either flavanol-rich dark chocolate or beverages made with cocoa powder experienced a small decrease in blood pressure.
